Target Audience

The target audience for the Chamber of Commerce Hawaii website would primarily be businesses and professionals who are located in Hawaii or interested in doing business in Hawaii. This could include local businesses of all sizes, entrepreneurs, investors, government officials, and individuals seeking to connect with the Hawaii business community. The website is designed to provide information and resources that can help businesses and professionals succeed in Hawaii's economy, so it may be particularly useful for those seeking to expand their network or learn about the latest trends and developments in the state's business environment.
