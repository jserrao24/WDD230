Design Principles

White space

Hacker.io

In the Hacker.io screenshot we can see white space and clean design applied. Our focal point is the Yeezy flip flops and all around it is a clean space that allow us to percibe better the product design. This helps to create a better experience for the user.

Visual Hierarchy

Birkenstock

Birkenstock site is a good example of visual hierarchy, the brand name is the first thing we notice, after that we see the opening announce and then we are directed to the call to action button. These steps allow the user to reach the conversions on the site.

Contrast

Urban Decay

In the Urban Decay site I could notice the group together the contrast principle between the background and typography colors and between the weight in the fonts.
