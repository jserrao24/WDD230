Discover Rivoli

Rivoli is a municipality in the Metropolitan City of Turin in the Italian region Piedmont of 48,798 inhabitants. Rivoli was established around the 1st century CE.

Line 1 of the Turin metro should reach Rivoli by the end of 2024 and be put into operation for people by 2025. More info on line 1...

Rivoli has a central position from all the important points of the Turin region. It is located about 15 km from Turin, and 17 km from Chiusa di San Michele, Venaria Reale, and Stupinigi Royal Hunting Lodge. It is also an hour's drive from Bardonecchia for skiing in winter and hiking in summer, and about 100 km from the French border.
