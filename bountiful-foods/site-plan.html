Purpose

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to provide information about the company, its products, and the location of its store and food trucks. It also has an interaction goal by allowing the user to create fruit drinks.

Target Audience


Audience

The general public is made up of local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas between the ages of 16 and 70. These are middle-class and upper-middle-class families and individuals. The audience is made up of both men and women regardless of their marital status. It includes people concerned with their communities or the planet as well as a healthy lifestyle.


Personas


Young Dynamic Executive

Fictional name: Maria Sanchez

Demographics: 25 years old, single childless

Goals and tasks: She feels concerned by her time and the Earth. She wants to play a positive role in the local economy as well as being healthy by playing sports and eating well. She is vegetarian and organic.

Environment: She lives in Carlsbad and works in San Diego. She uses both her mobile and her pc daily with a high quality Internet.


Upper Middle Class Family

Fictional name: Anderson Family

Demographics: Andrew & Jane: 45 - Elisa: 16 - Aaron: 12

Goals and tasks: The whole family is concerned with the Earth. The mother and the oldest daughter are very attached to a healthy diet. The kids love fun and fresh food. The father loves hearty food. They like to go to the beach on weekends and get refreshments there as well as lunch.

Environment:The family lives in Phoenix, Arizona. The whole family has a mobile phone. Everyone except Aaron has a personal pc. Aaron has a tablet. They use the Internet daily with high quality.


Scenarios


Young Dynamic Executive:
Where can I find local, healthy and organic food?
Where can I find a store that provides ready-made fruit and vegetable baskets?

Upper Middle Class Family

Which beach food truck uses glass glasses?
Which food truck has healthy and fun refreshments?
Which food truck allows you to eat heartily and healthily?
